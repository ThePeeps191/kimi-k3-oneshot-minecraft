ABOUT

VoxelVale is a pocket-sized voxel wilderness that lives entirely in this one web page. Hills, oceans, caves and forests are generated procedurally from a seed; every block you break or place is remembered in your browser's local storage, so your world is waiting for you when you come back.

TIPS

  • Dig straight into a hillside to find caves — and watch for coal, iron and gold ore.
  • The world auto-saves every 25 seconds, and whenever you pause or leave the page.
  • Sand shorelines mark the ocean; swim with Space.

BLOCKS

Click a block to load it into the highlighted hotbar slot — the selector then hops to the next slot. Press E to close.

DESKTOP NEEDED

VoxelVale is a first-person game that needs a keyboard, mouse and pointer lock — things touch devices can't offer yet. Please open this page on a desktop or laptop browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox or Safari) to play.
